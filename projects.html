Only Informed

A web platform designed to promote informed discussion on news articles. Users must pass an automated quiz about the article's content to comment, ensuring engagement is based on comprehension. Users with perfect scores can also rate the accuracy of article titles. The project integrates Flask for backend development, SQLAlchemy for database management, and automated systems for article fetching, quiz generation, and user interaction.
